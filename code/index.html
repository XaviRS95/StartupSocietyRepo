MTU Startup Society

Student society for people interested in startups and building them in the future



About the society

We are a group of MTU Cork students passionate about startups

This society is for you, if you are:

- Interested in startups

- Would like to know how to build your own startup in the future

- Would like to work at startup in the future

What are we planning to do as a society?

1. Lectures

Business professionals from top tier Irish start-ups are invited to share ideas, teach and inspire students as well as open them up to possible career choices in the tech start-up industry or to work on their start-ups


2. Workshops

Activities run by professionals working at start-ups, with a group of students to improve their skillset.


3. Company visits

Business professionals from top tier Irish start-ups are invited to share ideas, teach and inspire students as well as open them up to possible career choices in the tech start-up industry or to work on their start-ups


4. Brainstorming sessions

Sessions where students are welcomed to discuss with each other all the building tech startup-related topics


What are we planning to do as a society?

1. Popularize the idea of creating start-ups
2. Equip students with knowledge on how to build a tech start-up company
3. Create a space where students interested in building their tech start-ups can network, share ideas and collaborate
