WHO WE ARE:

LECTURES

Business professionals from top tier Irish start-ups are invited to share ideas, teach and inspire students as well as open them up to possible career choices in the tech start-up industry or to work on their start-ups


WORKSHOPS

Activities run by professionals working at start-ups, with a group of students to improve their skillset.

COMPANY VISITS

We give students the opportunity to see how tech start- ups operate internally. This benefits the company too by increasing their exposure to students which attracts young talent.

BRAINSTORMING SESSIONS

Sessions where students are welcomed to discuss with each other all the building tech startup-related topics
