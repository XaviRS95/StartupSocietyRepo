WHO WE ARE:

Student society for people interested in tech startups and building them in the future!

We are a group of MTU Cork students passionate about startups. This society is for you, if you are:

  • Interested in startups
  • Would like to know how to build your own startup in the future
  • Would like to work at startup in the future

What are we planning to do as a society?

1. Lectures

  • Lectures:

    • invited to share ideas, teach and inspire students as well as open them up to possible career choices in the tech start-up industry or to work on their start-ups

2. Workshops Activities run by professionals working at start-ups, with a group of students to improve their skillset. 3. Company visits We give students the opportunity to see how tech start- ups operate internally. This benefits the company too by increasing their exposure to students which attracts young talent. 4. Brainstorming sessions Sessions where students are welcomed to discuss with each other all the building tech startup-related topics
